Introduction

Masters of Business Administration in short MBA is a globally recognized masters degree. MBA comprises a number of subjects that are collectively known as Science of Management. MBA is one of the most popular and renowned degrees in the world. MBAs are highly respected in every corner of the world. MBA has been the first choice for students in Nepal who want to make their career in the management and administration sector. Masters in Business Administration is the first choice of management students who want to make their career in business administration and management be it for other companies or starting their own startup company and managing it. MBA degree provides graduates/students with endless capabilities and expertise to run, manage and handle businesses and other factors that are involved in running businesses.

MBA is intended for those who work in business and management and seeks career advancement, business ownership, or technical skills and business knowledge. Management areas include Human Resource Management, Operations Management, Marketing Management, Information Management, Financial Management, Strategic Management and Organizational Behaviour. The average fee for MBA in Nepal is 4 lakhs to 6 lakhs depending upon the college infrastructure and affiliation. The MBA under TU is relatively cheaper in comparison with other Universities MBA programs in Nepal.

Types/specializations of MBA program and related degrees in Nepal

MBA is a broad discipline and a branch of Management which covers many different things related to business and Administration. With the latest development of different theories, technologies and advancement and change in the ways of doing things, MBA program is also made more flexible and specialized to cover the latest development and broadness in the Management and Administration. In a very general perspective, MBA is broadly divided into two categories; general MBA and Executive MBA (EMBA). But on the basis of speciality (major/concentration) and/or mode of delivery, the MBA program has been named or classified into different forms. Some of the common forms of MBA degree in Nepal that are common are: Evening MBA, Weekend MBA, MBA (Global Business), MBA (Finance), MBA (marketing), Online MBA to name a few.

In addition to the more common specializations, like economics, finance, and marketing, other specialization of MBA program include:

  • Healthcare Management (MBA in Health Care Management)
  • Business Management (MBA in Business Management)
  • Human Resources (MBA in Human Resources (HR))
  • Marketing (MBA in Marketing)
  • Communication (MBA in Communication)
  • Project Management (MBA in Project Management)
  • Entrepreneurship (MBA in Entrepreneurship)
  • MBA in Economics
  • Global Management MBA in Global Management
  • MBA in strategic/risk management
  • MBA in IT Management

These are the general specialization of MBA which are widely popular these days. However, many of the popular MBA specializations are still new in Nepal and not yet implemented in the course structure of MBA colleges of Nepal. In many cases, you may also have the choice not to specialize. In this case, you receive an MBA without a specialization attached. This is also referred to as a General MBA.

Executive MBA in Nepal (EMBA and colleges that conduct EMBA course in Nepal)

The Executive MBA which is widely known as EMBA program has been among the most dynamic and fastest-growing programs for business professionals as they help to expand their education and knowledge to take on higher roles within their organization. Also, the EMBA program help executives keep themselves updated with the new management techniques and trends that arise with a change in time and structures of business models. All Executive MBA programs are meant for students who are serious about their studies and career at the same time. Most students in the EMBA program have five or more yearsâ€™ experience in management positions.

Various colleges in Kathmandu offer executive MBA (EMBA) programs. These programs are designed for ambitious professionals who have been working in middle to a senior level position in relevant fields. Work experience is compulsory to enrol in the Executive MBA (EMBA) program. Some say that EMBA classes are not as deep as the average MBA class, but others argue the classes are faster pace, but all the same material is covere

The classes for the EMBA program are run on a convenient modular basis, ideal for busy executives and active jobholders. For example, In KUSOM, EMBA classes run full day at Saturdays and one or two evenings on Sunday, Monday, Tuesday ( 5.30 pm to 8.30 pm). Other institutes also have similar flexible course hours.

The average cost to complete MBA in Nepal is between 4-7 lakhs. Many organizations sponsor their employees (generally management employees) to take this program in exchange for the agreed duration of service from them.

All About Evening MBA course

Evening MBA or MBA evening is the MBA program which is designed for students who want to continue their studies along with job or other engagements during the daytime. Basically the classes are conducted in the evening from around in-between 5 pm to 9 pm with slightly different schedules depending on colleges. The course is covered by this program is almost identical to the regular MBA. To cover the reduced class hours, evening MBA is of longer duration than other MBA. In Nepal, almost all evening MBA programs are around 3 years of duration.

In Nepal MBA is running under two categories:

  • By taking affiliation from domestic Universities like TU, Kathmandu University, Purbanchal University, Pokhara University etc.
  • By taking affiliation from foreign Universities

Online MBA or distant MBA , weekends MBA are not much popular in Nepal.

Scope of MBA in Nepal and Abroad

There is a bright future prospect of MBA in Nepal. The scope of MBA is vast and expanding in all areas of business and management. MBA graduates are hired to manage and run all sort of businesses and organisations like profit-seeking industries, Not for Profit Organisations (NGOs), government offices, financial institutions (banking, insurance, stockbroker firms), international projects etc. With the slight increase in development activities and signals of a boost in the economy, MBA graduates are starting to enjoy a good salary and career enhancements.

With the increased competition and expand in the private sector in the economy , each organisation be it small or big require a good administrator and manager to stand out in the competition. These level of administrative skills and managerial skills are the main charm of MBA graduates hence increasing the sell-ability in the local market. So the scope of MBA in Nepal in terms of career prospects and professional development is increasing and expanding.
